About Me

I'm a quantitative analyst specialising in data-driven trading and algorithmic strategy design. My background combines rigorous quantitative methods with hands-on trading experience, spanning market microstructure, portfolio optimisation, stochastic volatility models, reinforcement learning, and high-performance C++ matching engines.

My degree from the University of York emphasised Applied Quantitative Analysis, equipping me with statistical modelling, programming and data science skills that underpin my advanced trading research.
Market Microstructure & Execution

Limit-order book simulators, Avellaneda–Stoikov and Almgren–Chriss models, high-frequency C++ matching engine, Kalman & cointegration pairs trading.

Stochastic Models & Derivatives

Black–Scholes, Monte Carlo pricing, Heston & rough Bergomi volatility, Greeks calculator, implied volatility surfaces, GARCH & stochastic volatility forecasting.

Portfolio & Risk Management

Mean-variance and risk parity optimisation, factor models, VaR/CVaR simulations, efficient frontiers, GARCH and risk metrics.

Machine Learning & RL
